About me

My name is Gilbert, I'm 22 years old and I live in Northern Italy. Actually I work in a factory as a metalworker, but lately I started thinking that I could do something different, so I started following courses about python and I had a lot of fun!

Despite nowadays, with the rise of AI, I've seen people take advantage of it to develop their ideas very fast, I wanted to start from the basic. Indeed, I believe that to learn something doing some hard work is always the best idea, so I decided that at least at the beginning I want to write personally all the rows of my code. In that way I can understand better what I'm doing and how everything works.

This is my first ever personal project. The inspiration arrived quite straightforward: by looking at a problem that a lot of people have at home. I wanted to create something simple to use, but with a lot of different sources of inspiration, I hope that you enjoyed it!
